Tucked one block off of the main street, Café Costa is a cozy little spot marrying a lively atmosphere with immaculate cups of coffee. This local coffee shop takes its cue from coastal warmth, with light wood décor, lush greenery, and an open, airy vibe. Café Costa proudly prides itself with rich coffee, delicious lattes, and a constantly changing menu. Equally perfect for both morning pickups and afternoon unwinding, it is generally favored because of its spots of calm, friendly service and, of course, a perfect cup of coffee.
